# Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected and how it is used.

## Information We Collect

Depending on enabled features, the bot may store:

* Discord User IDs
* Discord Server IDs
* Discord Channel IDs
* Discord Role IDs
* Giveaway data
* Modmail conversation data
* Sticky message configurations
* Repeating message configurations
* Vanity role configurations

The bot does not collect Discord passwords, email addresses, or payment information.

## How We Use Data

Data is used solely to provide the bot's functionality, including:

* Processing modmail conversations
* Managing vanity role systems
* Running giveaways
* Delivering sticky messages
* Delivering scheduled messages
* Saving server-specific configuration

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share collected data with third parties except where required by law.

## Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary to provide the bot's services. Server administrators may request removal of server-specific configuration data.

## Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect stored data. However, no method of electronic storage is completely secure.

## Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the bot after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

## Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact the bot's support server or development team.
